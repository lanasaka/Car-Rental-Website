What do you need to rent a car?


To rent a car from Enterprise Rent-A-Car in the United States and Canada, you will need the following:

To be 25 years old


(if you’re under 25, you may be required to pay a young driver’s fee, which varies by rental location)

A valid driver's license with a photo



A photo ID


(For example: a driver’s license or passport)



Credit or debit card


(check the acceptable forms of payment at the location you’re renting from)
